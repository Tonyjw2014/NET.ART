Ephemeral Threads: Navigating Digital Transience

Curated by Jiawei Zhang

Curatorial Statement

This exhibition delves into the impermanent and often fleeting aspects of digital existence. Through the lens of diverse artists, we explore the ways digital mediums and messages both appear and disappear within our contemporary culture.

Chill Space - Daniel Leyva (2010)

The Collapse of PAL - Rosa Menkman (2009)

Data Diaries - Cory Arcangel (2003)

Open Air - Rafael Lozano-Hemmer (2012)

Text Rain - Camille Utterback (1999)
