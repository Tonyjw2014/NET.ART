Chill Space - Daniel Leyva (2010)

Chill space, a sort of emoji pattern that is miraculously put together and forgotten that shined for a moment in time to deliver an intricate feeling to the viewer, of our fading youth, often forgotten like this art to its owner “Chill Space was Leyva's contribution to JstChillin.org's Serial Chillers series. You are presented with an expansive field composed of HTML form elements. Complete the form with your name and personal URL, manipulate a few form elements, and submit to receive your own custom created animated gif Chill Space.” Learn more about the artwork here.
