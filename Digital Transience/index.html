Ephemeral Threads: Navigating Digital Transience

Curated by Jiawei Zhang

Curatorial Statement

In "Ephemeral Threads: Navigating Digital Transience," we delve into the fleeting essence of digital existence, showcasing works that embody the transient nature of our digital lives. This exhibition, curated by Jiawei Zhang, presents a thought-provoking collection from artists Daniel Leyva, Rosa Menkman, and Brenna Murphy, each exploring the ephemeral aspects of digital culture through their unique lenses.

Chill Space - Daniel Leyva (2010)

An immersive digital environment evolving over time, inviting users to experience the serene, transient nature of digital landscapes.

The Collapse of PAL - Rosa Menkman (2009)

A pioneering work in glitch art, exploring the transient beauty of data corruption and the decay of digital communication systems.

lattice~face~stack - Brenna Murphy (2012)

A digital sculpture at the intersection of digital and physical, exploring the fluid identity of digital entities and the ephemeral nature of virtual spaces.
